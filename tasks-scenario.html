Tasks & Scenarios for KidDose Evaluation

This section outlines the specific tasks and scenarios that were used during our heuristic evaluation to assess the usability of the KidDose pediatric medication management application.

Evaluation Context

The following tasks were designed to cover key functionality and common user journeys within the KidDose app. Evaluators examined the interface while mentally working through these tasks to identify potential usability issues for both parent and pediatrician user roles.

Task 1

Sign Up for an Account

  • Open the app and navigate to the Sign-Up page.
  • Fill in the required information such as full name, email, password, and any other necessary details.
  • Enter the verification code.
  • Tap the Sign Up button.
Task 2

Scan a Medicine Name and Check Its Details

  • Tap the Scan Medicine button from the center of bottom navigation.
  • Use the camera to scan the medicine name.
  • Wait for the app to process and display the medicine details.
  • Review the displayed information such as name, dosage, and usage instructions.
  • Click Done after reviewing.
Task 3

Change Account Password

  • Go to the Profile Settings section.
  • Click Password input field.
  • Enter the verification code.
  • Create new password and confirm.
  • Tap Save Changes.
